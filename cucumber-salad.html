Smashed Asian Cucumber Salad

A crisp, high-performance side dish with zero stovetop latency. Perfect for cooling down a spicy main build.

Chef's Note: This dish acts as nature's water-cooling system (hydration) with a spicy capsaicin kick to boost metabolic throughput.

🛒 Ingredients Checklist

  • 2 Large English Cucumbers
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt (The Debugger)
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Chinkiang Black Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Chili Crisp
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced

👨‍🍳 Deployment Steps

1

Smash Assets: Place cucumbers in a bag and hit them with a rolling pin until they split. Irregular edges hold more dressing.

2

Drain Buffer: Toss with salt and let sit for 10 mins. Drain the excess liquid to prevent a "watery UI."

3

Inject Dependencies: Whisk the vinegar, soy sauce, oil, and chili crisp. Pour over cucumbers and serve cold.
