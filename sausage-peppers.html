🍳 Rustic Skillet Sausage & Peppers

A savory, caramelized blend of juicy sausages and tender-crisp peppers. Perfect on its own, in a hoagie roll, or over a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausages (4-5 links)
  • Green Bell Peppers (2 large, sliced into strips)
  • Onion (1 medium, sliced into half-moons)
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)
  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)
  • Salt & Black Pepper (to taste)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1 Sear the Sausages

Place a large skillet over medium-high heat with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Add the whole sausages. Cook for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are browned on all sides. They don't need to be fully cooked through yet.

2 Sauté the Aromatics

Remove the sausages from the pan and set them aside on a plate. In the same pan (keep those flavorful juices!), add the remaining oil, the sliced green peppers, and the onions. Sauté for about 6-8 minutes until the edges are softened and slightly charred.

3 Combine and Simmer

Stir in the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using). Sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Slice the browned sausages into 1-inch coins and add them back into the skillet.

4 The Final Melt

Turn the heat down to medium-low. Season with salt and pepper. Cover the pan and let it cook for another 5 minutes to ensure the sausages are cooked through and the flavors have married together.

🥖 Serving Suggestion: This is incredible served inside a toasted sub roll with a little provolone cheese, or kept "low-carb" by eating it straight out of a bowl with a fork.
