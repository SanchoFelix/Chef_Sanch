🍳 Rustic Skillet Sausage & Peppers

This classic Italian-American staple is the ultimate weeknight "win." By searing the sausages whole first, we lock in the juices, while the peppers and onions caramelize in the rendered fats to create a natural, rich sauce.

🌿 Why this is good for you

Green peppers are a massive source of Vitamin C. Adding fresh jalapeños provides capsaicin, which can boost metabolism and heart health.

380Calories
22gProtein
12gCarbs
28gFat

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausages (5 links)
  • Green Bell Peppers (2 large)
  • Onion (1 large yellow)
  • Garlic (Minced)
  • 🌶️ Jalapeños

Step-by-Step Instructions

1 Prep and Sear

Slice the onions and peppers into strips. Sear the sausages whole for 5-7 minutes until they have a beautiful golden crust.

2 Sauté the Aromatics

Remove sausages. Toss in the peppers, onions, and jalapeños. Sauté until softened and charred.

3 Flavor Integration

Add the minced garlic to the center of the pan until fragrant. Slice the sausages into coins and reintroduce them to the skillet.

4 The Final Simmer

Let everything cook together for 5 minutes until the sauce coats every piece of sausage and pepper.

🥖 Serving Suggestion: Your batch looks incredibly juicy—serve it over toasted sourdough or in a hoagie roll to soak up all those pan juices!
