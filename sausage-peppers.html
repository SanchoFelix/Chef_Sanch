Sausage & Peppers Chef Sanch Gem

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausages
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Onions & Garlic
  • Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper

Instructions

1. Sear sausages in a skillet until brown.

2. Sauté peppers and onions until soft.

3. Combine, slice sausages, and simmer for 5 mins.
