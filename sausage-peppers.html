🍳 Rustic Skillet Sausage & Peppers

This classic Italian-American staple is the ultimate weeknight "win." By searing the sausages whole first, we lock in the juices, while the green peppers and onions caramelize in the rendered fats to create a natural, rich sauce. It's a colorful, fragrant dish that feels fancy but only requires one pan and basic kitchen skills.

🌿 Why this is good for you

Green peppers are a massive source of Vitamin C (even more than oranges!) and antioxidants. Using onions and garlic provides prebiotic fiber to support gut health. For a leaner version, chicken or turkey Italian sausages offer high protein with lower saturated fat.

380 Calories
22g Protein
12g Carbs
28g Fat

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausages (4-5 links)
  • Green Bell Peppers (2 large, sliced into strips)
  • Onion (1 medium, sliced into half-moons)
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)
  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Red Pepper Flakes, Salt & Black Pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

1 Sear the Sausages

Place a large skillet over medium-high heat with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Add the whole sausages. Cook for 5-7 minutes until browned. Set aside.

2 Sauté the Aromatics

Add remaining oil and sauté peppers and onions for 6-8 minutes until soft and slightly charred.

3 Combine and Simmer

Add garlic, slice sausages into coins, and return to pan. Toss well.

4 The Final Melt

Cover and simmer for 5 minutes. Season to taste and serve hot.

🥖 Serving Suggestion: Serve in a toasted roll with provolone, or over cauliflower rice for a low-carb masterpiece.
