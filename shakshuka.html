One-Pan Chickpea Shakshuka

A rustic, 30-minute high-protein poach. Rustic vibes, modern performance.

The Macro-Stack

380Calories
18gProtein
42gCarbs
14gFat
💪 Health Benefits: This dish is a plant-based protein powerhouse. The simmered tomatoes are rich in Lycopene, a potent antioxidant that becomes more bioavailable when cooked with the healthy fats in olive oil.

Ingredients Checklist

  • 1 Onion (diced)
  • 1 Red bell pepper (diced)
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 can (15 oz) Chickpeas (drained)
  • 1 can (14 oz) Crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Cumin & 1 tsp Smoked paprika
  • 3-4 Large Eggs
  • Feta cheese & Fresh Parsley

Implementation Steps

  • 1Sauté Veggies

    Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium. Add onion, pepper, and garlic, cooking 4–5 minutes until soft. This builds our flavor base—think of it as setting up your environment variables.

  • 2Simmer Sauce

    Stir in spices, tomato paste, tomatoes, and chickpeas. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens and the flavors integrate.

  • 3Poach Eggs

    Create small wells in the sauce. Crack eggs into the wells, cover, and cook for 5-8 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny. Don't over-process here; let the steam do the work.

  • 4Finish & Serve

    Garnish with feta and fresh herbs. Serve with crusty bread for dipping. Deployment complete.

    • 💡 Pro-Tips for Success

    • Substitutions: Use butter beans if chickpeas are out of stock.
    • Heat: Add harissa paste for a spicy backend upgrade.
    • Additions: Fold in spinach or kale just before adding the eggs.
