🍗 Honey Garlic Glazed Chicken

The magic of this dish lies in the Maillard reaction—the browning of the chicken combined with the caramelization of raw honey. It creates a sticky, umami-rich glaze that coats the protein perfectly. This is a 15-minute deployment that beats takeout every single time.

🌿 Why this is good for you

Garlic contains allicin, known for its immune-boosting properties. Using honey provides a natural energy source, and when paired with lean chicken breast, you get a high-protein meal that keeps you satiated without the heavy oils found in processed glazes.

320 Calories
34g Protein
24g Carbs
8g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken Breast (cut into 1-inch pieces)
  • 2 tbsp All-purpose Flour
  • 1/3 cup Honey
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce (Low Sodium)
  • 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 3-4 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • Optional: Ginger, Sesame Seeds, Green Onions

Step-by-Step Instructions

1 Prep and Coat

Pat chicken dry with paper towels. Toss in a bowl with flour, salt, and pepper until lightly coated. This creates the "anchor" for our sauce to stick to.

2 Whisk the Glaze

In a small bowl, combine honey, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and ginger. Whisk until smooth.

3 Sear the Protein

Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high. Brown chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until golden. Work in batches to avoid crowding!

4 Glaze and Thicken

Lower heat to medium. Pour sauce over chicken. Let it bubble for 2-4 minutes until it transforms into a thick, shiny glaze.

🍚 Serving Suggestion: Best served over a steaming bed of jasmine rice with a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
